07 / 08  ·  Key Takeaways

KEY TAKEAWAYS

📋

Pick the right format

Chronological, functional or combination. Match it to your experience level.

🔗

Add your portfolio

Include a link to your work. Let employers see what you can do before the interview.

🏅

List certifications

They show passion and commitment to growing your skills outside of school.

✍️

Write a summary

A short paragraph at the top introducing who you are and why you're the right fit.
